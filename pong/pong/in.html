Hello! Welcome to Pong!

To play, use the WASD keys on the keyboard to control the left paddle and the ⬆ ⬇ ⬅ ➡ keys on the keyboard to control the right paddle!
You can press the spacebar on the keyboard to pause the game at any time.
Be warned that with every death, the faster the ball gets!
