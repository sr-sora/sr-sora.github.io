Hoshi

Their once was long ago a child named Hoshi. Every night, they would diligently gaze at the night sky and revel under the thousands of lights hung in the sky.
They also loved to fold paper hearts, as there was an old saying in the village that to replace the fallen stars from the sky, one must fold paper stars. So fold and fold Hoshi did.
One fateful night, Hoshi realized that there were only a few stars out that night. The jar of stars disappeared! It seemed that the jar had been knocked open and then blown away by the wind. Grief-striken, Hoshi set out to find their paper stars and return the night sky to it's former glory.
